About Us

Aim of most NGOs is socialization where public service is merely a cosmetic affair which has no deep impact on society. However, fellowship in the Parishad is only a means to an end and not the end. Activities of Bharat Vikas Parishad are comprehensive and aim at multifaceted development of the society combining equally both sanskar and sewa aspects with capacity building of those served. Bharat Vikas Parishad is a service-cum-sanskar oriented non-political socio-cultural family-based voluntary organisation, where both husband and wife join as members to serve the society. The members of the Parishad meet their own expenses while participating in its activities and also contribute towards administrative expenses of the organisation. Funds collected for projects and programmes are kept separately and are spent 100 per cent exclusively for the designated purposes. Parishad lays emphasis on developing the competence of the beneficiaries through their involvement so that they are no longer dependent on charity and lead a dignified and honourable life essentially unchanged.

User Friendly

Bharat Ko Jano is a voluntary organization where citizens from all walks of life join to work for the development and growth of our country in all fields of human endeavour through promoting a sense of patriotism, national unity and integrity and culminating in Swasth-Samarth-Sanskarit Bharat i.e. physically, economically and morally strong India. Our work can be broadly categoried into Service (sewa) and Educational (sanskar).

Flexibility

Our Sanskar projects are for the students, youth, women and the elderly. For the students, we organise Bharat Ko Jano quiz competition and chorus singing of patriotic songs competition and organise programmes in schools where students pay respect of their teachers. Students from colleges and professional institutes also participate in online Bharat Ko Jano quiz programmes. We organise simple mass marriages of poor young girls and boys and sanskar shivirs for the students, women and the elderly and seminars for the youth.
